# Module 05 – Citation & Reference Extractor

**Purpose:** Identify and surface important citations and references from the paper.

**Responsibilities:**
- Scan the text for main cited works (for example, author–year references or named methods).
- Internally track which citations are most important for understanding the paper.
- Pass helpful citation information to:
  - The Mini-Glossary (for terms linked to specific works).
  - The Checks & Warnings section, if citations are unclear or missing.
- Never invent or guess citations that are not actually present in the paper.

**Passes to:** Module 04 – Rendering & Refinement (for incorporation into summaries and glossary).
