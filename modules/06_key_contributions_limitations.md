# Module 06 – Key Contributions & Limitations

**Purpose:** Highlight the main contributions and limitations of the research paper.

**Responsibilities:**
- Read the section summaries and original text to identify the paper’s core contributions.
- Identify any limitations, assumptions, or open problems described by the authors.
- Make sure each contribution or limitation is clearly supported by the text (no guessing).
- Feed these points into:
  - The overall Paper Summary.
  - The Expert Summary.
  - The Lay Summary.
  - The Checks & Warnings section, if the paper has major gaps or unclear claims.

**Passes to:** Module 04 – Rendering & Refinement for integration into final outputs.
