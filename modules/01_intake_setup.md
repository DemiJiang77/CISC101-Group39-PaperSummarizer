# Module 01 – Intake & Setup

**Purpose:** Collect and normalize all inputs for the Research Paper Summarizer.

**Responsibilities:**
- Gather the full text for each section of the paper from the user.
- Collect an ordered section list (e.g., Abstract, Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion).
- Ask for or receive the audience type (expert or general).
- Normalize section names so they are consistent internally.
- Detect missing, empty, or very short sections (for example, sections under 50 words).
- Package all inputs into a clear internal structure for later modules.

**Passes to:** Module 02 – Section Loop.
